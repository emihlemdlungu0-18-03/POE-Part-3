About Beauty Studio

Our Story

Beauty Studio was founded on the belief that professional makeup should be accessible to everyone, whether you're preparing for a major event or a private photoshoot. We are also welcoming those people who feel like they belong on this type of business to come have lessons with us with professionals so that they could achieve their goals.

Our Mission

Our organization aims to showcase professional makeup artistry to a wider audience. We believe that when a client feels confident in their look, they shine in every environment. We value aesthetics because we have different types of clients that value that and like taking content we allow that while also making sure that every client is feeling good coming to us and just being in a comfortable environment.

Our Vision

As an organisation we would like to become the premier industry leader in Beauty, being recognised always the first choice for professional makeup, lashes, and microbalding. We strive to be the top of mind brand for both high end consumers and aspiring artist.
